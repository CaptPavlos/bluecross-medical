## Overview

This document compiles maritime medical regulations for 69 flag states based on Paris MOU 2024 performance data. The information covers:

- **Medical chest categories** (A, B, C based on voyage distance)
- **Inspection requirements** and intervals
- **TMAS contacts** for telemedical assistance
- **Equipment requirements** including AED mandates
- **Yacht-specific regulations**
- **Training requirements** (STCW Medical First Aid/Medical Care)

## Paris MOU Classification System

### White List (Quality Flags)
- Consistently low detention rates
- Strong PSC (Port State Control) performance
- Priority for inspections every 24-36 months

### Grey List (Average Performance)
- Moderate detention rates
- Standard inspection intervals (12-24 months)

### Black List (High Risk)
Risk categories within Black List:
- **Medium Risk**: Enhanced inspections
- **Medium-High Risk**: Priority inspections, potential port access refusal
- **High Risk**: Mandatory expanded inspections
- **Very High Risk**: Near-certain detention and banning risk
